How does the EuroLeague Calculator work?

Our EuroLeague Calculator and Simulator is an advanced mathematical tool designed to predict Playoff and Play-In scenarios. By using the head-to-head (H2H) history, official tiebreaker rules, and current season statistics, we generate real-time standings.

The predictive engine includes a Monte Carlo simulation model. By running 2,000 simulations of the regular season, the algorithm processes the teams' form (last 5 games), home-court advantage, and weighted win percentages using the Log5 formula. This allows us to provide accurate statistical probabilities on which teams will clinch the coveted Top 6, who will fall into the Play-In, and who will be eliminated.
